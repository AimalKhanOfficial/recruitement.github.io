We at Tip Top Recruitment pick the best, so there are no chances!

At Tip Top Recruitment we don’t take risks, we use in-depth research from the Tip Top Recruitment Institute and rely on our experience of the local market to find the right people for your business.

To tailor candidates, we’ll meet with you to understand your organisation and employment needs.

Then we’ll select candidates for you using a combination of psychometric testing, screening and competency interviews. We’ll also carry out full CV and work history checks.

Finding new talent is just the start of what we do. In a constantly changing world, our clients need to know what is happening. That’s why we produce a Tip Top Recruitment Survey to keep you up-to-date with current employment trends, salary levels and new legislation.
